# Roadmap

## High priority — before next tournament

### 1. Login persistence & authentication
Sessions survive page refresh; protected routes; user identity tied to picks and scores.

### 2. Draft table — 4-column layout with snake order
Participant | Rd 1 | Rd 2 | Rd 3 columns; snake fill order visualized as picks are made.

### 3. Last pick display
Persistent banner or sidebar showing the most recently drafted golfer and who picked them.

### 4. Enter key to confirm golfer selection
Keyboard submit in the golfer search/select input; no click required.

---

## Mid priority — during tournament

### 5. Leaderboard position movement indicators
Green ▲ / red ▼ arrows with delta vs previous round's final standings.

---

## Post-tournament / next cycle

### 6. Tournament history
Archive of past tournaments — winners, picks, final standings, scores.

### 7. Side bets area
Create, track, and settle prop bets among participants during a tournament.
